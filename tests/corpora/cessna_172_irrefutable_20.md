## Fault 1 — Seat Rail Locking Mechanism Failure

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| **AD Number** | 2008-05-09 |
| **Effective** | April 8, 2008 |
| **Models** | 172, 182, 206 series (172R, 172S, etc.) |
| **Unsafe Condition** | Seat slippage or seat roller housing departing the seat rail, preventing pilot from reaching controls |
| **Mandated Fix** | Inspect seat rails for dirt/debris, measure locking pin engagement (min 0.15"), inspect hole diameter (max 0.42"), inspect rollers for flat spots. Repetitive every 100 hrs or 12 months |
| **Source URL** | federalregister.gov (AD 2008-05-09) |
| **Mechanic Query** | "The pilot seat slides unexpectedly during flight. It won't lock in position. What should I check?" |
| **Expected Gus Diagnosis** | Seat rail locking mechanism — inspect pin engagement depth, rail hole wear, roller condition per AD 2008-05-09 |
